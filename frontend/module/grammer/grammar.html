How to use Grammar Check

Fix spelling, grammar, and clarity in your assignments or notes.

1
Paste or type your text

Enter your paragraph, essay, or notes in the left panel. Works best with complete sentences.

2
Optional: Convert to Notes

Tick Convert to Notes if you want cleaned-up bullet-style study notes instead of a straight grammar fix.

3
Run the check

Click Check Grammar. Corrected text appears on the right with an issue percentage.

4
Review mistakes

Use Show Mistakes to highlight what changed, then Copy the final text to your document.

Tip: For long lecture transcripts, run Grammar Check after using the Summarizer or Transcript tools.
