How to use AI Quiz Generator

Quick MCQ practice from any text — ideal for revision on the spot.

1
Add your content

Paste notes in the text box or click Import File to load a TXT, PDF, or DOCX file.

2
Set the number of MCQs

Choose how many multiple-choice questions you want (5–20). The AI picks important points from your material.

3
Generate & attempt

Click Generate Quiz. Questions appear on the right — select an option for each MCQ and get instant feedback.

4
Review and retry

Use Clear to start over with new content, or regenerate after editing your notes for a fresh set of questions.

Tip: For full exam-style papers with short/long questions, Word download, or timed tests, use Student Generate Paper in the Question Bank module.
