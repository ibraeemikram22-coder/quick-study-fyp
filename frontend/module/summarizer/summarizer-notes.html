      How to use AI Summarized Notes

      Compress long lectures or chapters into clear, study-ready notes.

      1
      Add your source

      Paste a transcript or chapter text, or Import File (TXT, PDF, DOCX). Large files are read in parts and merged.

      2
      Adjust settings

      Pick language, content type (general or math), output format, and how many bullet points you need. Leave Quick mode off to summarize the whole file.

      3
      Generate summary

      Click Generate Summary and watch the progress bar. Notes appear on the right with word-count stats.

      4
      Copy or download

      Use What changed? to see removed fluff and key terms kept. Copy or download the final notes for revision.

      Tip: Pair this with Video Transcript — transcribe a lecture first, then paste the text here for short notes.
