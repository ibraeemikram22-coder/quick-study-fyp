How to use AI Content Humanizer

Make AI-written text sound natural and human — useful for essays, reports, and assignments.

1
Paste AI-generated text

Put your ChatGPT or other AI output in the left box. Minimum 20 words, maximum 500 words per run.

2
Click Humanize

The tool rewrites stiff, repetitive phrasing into smoother, more natural sentences while keeping the meaning.

3
Check the result

Read the output on the right. Word counts and rewrite stats show how much the text was adjusted.

4
Copy and use

Click Copy Output when satisfied. Use Clear to start with new text.

Tip: Always read the humanized text once before submitting — small factual details should still be checked by you.
