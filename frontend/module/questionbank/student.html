How to use Student Generate Paper

Turn your notes, PDF, or DOCX into a custom paper in a few steps.

1
Pick a mode first

Practice paper gives you a clean question sheet for Word or printing. Timed test lets you attempt the paper online with a live timer.

2
Add your study material

Upload a PDF, DOCX, or TXT file — or paste your chapter or lecture notes. The AI reads this content to build relevant questions.

3
Choose question types & counts

Toggle MCQ, Short, and Long questions and set how many of each you want. In timed mode, an estimated duration appears automatically.

4
Generate and use your paper

Practice mode: download Word (questions only), print, or save. Timed mode: answer on screen, submit when done, then review your MCQ score and the answer key.

Tip: This module is different from the Quiz tool — here you get full papers (MCQ + short + long), Word export, and optional timed self-tests. Formal board headers are added on the Teacher dashboard.
