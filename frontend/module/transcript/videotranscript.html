How to use Video Transcript

Get a readable text version of any YouTube video or uploaded lecture clip.

1
Choose input type

Use YouTube Link for online videos, or Upload Video for a local file (max 20 MB).

2
Start transcription

Paste the URL or drop your file, then click generate. YouTube captions are used when available for faster, accurate results.

3
Read the output

Lines follow the spoken order in Roman Urdu / Roman English — easy to read, not Hindi script.

4
Copy or download

Use the transcript in Summarizer for short notes, or in Quiz / Student Paper to build questions from the lecture.

Note: Copyright-blocked or unavailable videos show a clear error message instead of a partial transcript.
